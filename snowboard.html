SnowBoard

Project Description

Focus - SnowBoard is an e-commerce website that sells snowboard gear.
User Persona - Sally, 28, Los Angeles, Corporate Employee
Behavior - Mobile user, frequently on social media, news outlets, and online shopping.
Needs - A sleek yet descriptive e-commerce website that sells snowboarding gear primarily to beginner-intermediate riders.
Pain Points - Uninformative snowboarding websites that prioritize promotions and specials, sacrificing legibility and a seamless user experience.
Story - “I enjoy snowboarding on my free weekends, but don’t have much time to dedicate to boarding outside of that.”
“I’m new to boarding but I’m excited to continue learning and improving.”
“My knowledge of snowboarding equipment is minimal, so package options with informative descriptions to purchase gear is important to me.”
“I’m fashionable and enjoy staying up-to-date with the latest styles and popular items.”

Pages & Features

Navigation - Logo, Links (Boards, Boots, Bindings, Gear), Search, Hamburger (Account, Cart, Help).
Footer - Contact, Services, Information, Social Media Links.
Main - Banner, Promo, Categories (Best Sellers, New Collections, Packages), Product Information (Brand, Board Name, Price).
Product Page - Banner, Promo, Product Information (Brand, Board Name, Price), Filters (Color, Size, Price, Name), Add To Cart, Related Boards.
Contact Page - Full Name, Email, Message, Submit.
Inspiration - Burton, Evo, Zumiez.
