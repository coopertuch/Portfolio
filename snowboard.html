SnowBoard

Project Description

SnowBoard is an e-commerce website that sells snowboard gear. Required pages include a home, product category, and product page. The navigation bar features include the SB logo, links (boards, boots, bindings, gear), search, and hamburger (account, cart, help). The footer features include contact, services, information, and social media links. The home page features include a banner, promo copy, categories (best sellers, new collections, packages), and product information (brand, board name, price). The product category page features include a banner, promo copy, search filters (most popular, color, size, price, name), and product information (brand, board name, price). The product page features include product information (brand, board name, price, description), add to cart options (quantity, colors, size, add to cart), and related products.

User Persona

Sally, 28, works as a corporate employee in Los Angeles. She’s constantly using her iPhone to keep in touch with co-workers and friends, read the news, and shop online. She shares, “I’m new to snowboarding but I’m excited to continue learning and improving. I drive up to the mountains on my free weekends, but don’t have much time to dedicate to boarding outside of that. I’m interested in purchasing my own gear now, but my knowledge of snowboarding equipment is minimal, so package options with informative descriptions is important to me. Looking good is equally important, so I want access to the latest styles and trends as well.” Sally needs a descriptive e-commerce website that showcases popular collections. The website should also be sleek and intuitive, careful not to sacrifice legibility and a seamless purchasing experience with pop-ups, excessive banners, or too many board options.
