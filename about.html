Cooper Tuch, UX & Frontend, Los Angeles

My career journey has taken me across disciplines, from media planning, to software engineering, and finally to user experience. While I got a taste of fusing my dual passion in psychology and technology in digital marketing, I knew I wanted to take it a step further and pursue a career in tech to create powerful tools and intuitive experiences for communities I admire.

There was a feeling of necessity to start contributing to the projects that have been foundational to many of my own passions, which led me to software engineering. Working as a frontend developer at a startup, my role focused on coding responsive web pages, but was broad enough to include user research, data analysis, and journey mapping, processes that I'm already very comfortable with.

This position introduced me to UX research and design; paired with web development and media planning, user experience proved to be the perfect intersection of my skills and passions. My focus now is to build intuitive software that supports any number of digital creators, aspiring musicians, relentless academics, design fanatics, game players, health advocates, and world travelers.

Fun Facts: I’ve completed 75+ escape rooms, solo traveled across North America, Europe, and Asia chasing food and music, and I’m passionate about DJing.
