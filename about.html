Cooper Tuch, UX & Frontend, Los Angeles

Having graduated California State University, Fullerton with a degree in Business Marketing, I began work as a Media Planner at a Digital Marketing Agency in 2018. While I enjoyed user research, data analysis, curating ads, and designing user flow to drive conversions, the desire to build the digital products that I was advertising grew.

This led me to the General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive program in 2019, where I learned Full Stack Web Development. I’m captivated by these technologies, and it was essential that I understood how the code that dictates how I interact with software works.

Following GA, I worked as a Frontend Developer, designing and building websites in 2020. Using a combination of skills from Media Planning and Web Development for these projects, I was able to discover the role that would prove to be a perfect intersection of my experiences - UX Research & Design.

I’m excited now to work on projects and build products for any of the industries that are important to me, including music, education, travel, food, fitness, nutrition, and health. Fun Facts: I’ve completed 75+ escape rooms, solo traveled across North America, Europe, and Asia chasing food and music, and I’m passionate about DJing.
