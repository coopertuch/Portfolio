Cooper Tuch, UX & Frontend, Los Angeles

My career journey has taken me across disciplines, from media planning, to software engineering, and finally to user experience. While my interest in technology and psychology sent me towards digital marketing, I knew that I still wanted more in the tech space.

There was a feeling of necessity to start contributing to the projects that have been foundational to many of my own passions, which led me to software engineering. Working as a frontend developer at a startup, my role focused on coding responsive web pages, but was broad enough to include user research, data analysis, and journey mapping, processes that I'm already very comfortable with.

This position introduced me to what is formally known as UX research and design; paired with web development and media planning, user experience proved to be the perfect intersection of my skills and passions. My focus now is to build intuitive software that supports any number of digital creators, aspiring musicians, relentless academics, design fanatics, game players, health advocates, and world travelers.

Fun Facts: I’ve completed 75+ escape rooms, solo traveled across North America, Europe, and Asia chasing food and music, and I’m passionate about DJing.
