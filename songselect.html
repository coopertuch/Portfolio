SongSelect
SongSelect is an e-commerce website that sells music files with licensed samples and synths for producers.


User Persona: Henry, 24, Los Angeles, Musician/Producer/DJ

Behavior: Desktop user, frequently using digital audio workspaces, DJ software, and streaming services.

Needs: A cloud platform to manage all songs, stems, and synths that will be used for production and DJ events, using a familiar interface and extensive filtering.

Pain Points: Song, stem, and synth files scattered across platforms and local folders. Difficulty managing and filtering libraries. Frustration buying files using a variety of payment and download methods.

Story: “I’m a DJ at various local bars, so having access to the hottest songs and stems for my own shows is essential.”

“I’m tired of spending hours downloading files from countless websites and organizing my library, I just want to produce!”

“If I’m not producing and on the move, I’ll be on my phone searching for new music on Spotify or Soundcloud.”

“My laptop is constantly out of storage, so I need a platform to store my files on the cloud.”
