SongSelect

SongSelect is an desktop application that licenses music files with licensed samples and synths for producers drawing inspiration from Spotify, BeatPort, Splice, and SoundCloud. As audio streaming services continue to sync with DJ softwares that allow for direct streaming of audio files, SongSelect serves as a Spotify redesign. While Spotify provides a sleek interface with easy drag and drop features, it lacks crucial song information such as soundwaves, key, and BPM. Any DJ or producer can attest that downloading music and managing libraries is unnecessarily tedious, so as licensing allows it, SongSelect will store all music in the cloud, eliminating the need to download each track, while simultaneously being the marketplace for samples, stems, and synths; gone are the days of scavenging the corners of the internet for an acapella.
