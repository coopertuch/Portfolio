MeditateApp

MeditateApp is an iOS application inspired by Calm, Headspace, and Meditopia for customizable meditations. The design focuses on simplifying these applications, saving users time by providing customizable meditation sessions instead of needing to search through content for the perfect option. Built for busy students, young professionals, or anyone looking for a convenient, simplified, personalized meditation option, this application, paired with tracking, optimizes for building new habits, removing the barriers users face when choosing to meditate. Users can customize the sound, focus, time, and toggle guided settings. Activity is stored on the users page, where they are able to see a variety of performance statistics.
