MeditateApp

Project

Focus - MeditateApp is a mobile iOS application for custom guided meditations.
User Persona - John, 18, New York, Student
Behavior - Desktop & mobile user, frequently on social media, listening to podcasts, and studying.
Needs - A mobile application for customizable meditation sessions, designed to be easy to use paired with activity tracking.
Pain Points - Overcomplicated meditation apps with content locked behind paywalls. Inability to customize the length and focus on sessions.
Story - “I’m still getting adjusted to living in the city and I’m having difficulty finding time to relax with my busy school schedule”
“I constantly use my bullet journal to track progression for my goals and activities.”
“I have some experience meditating, and I often switch between guided and unguided sessions.”

Features

Navigation - Logo, Account.
Footer - Start Button.
Main - Banner, Meditation Settings (Sound, Voice, Time, Focus).
Account - User Information (Full Name, Email, Password), Meditation Statistics (Total Sessions, Minutes, Avg. Sessions, Daily Activity), Help Button.
Inspiration - Calm, Headspace, Meditopia.
