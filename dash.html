Dash

Dash is an e-commerce website inspired by an “exciting, fresh, and convenient” theme. The design focuses on vibrant images, bright color themes, and playful yet professional font types. Meal prep services are often limited in scope when it comes to meal options, so Dash highlights international flavors and informs customers of nutritional facts and sourcing. Dash is ideal for those who enjoy eating fun foods but want to maintain a clean diet, energy, and convenience.
